How to use this dashboard
Price Colors
GreenUp from yesterday — positive selling signal
RedDown from yesterday — monitor closely
AmberFlat — little change from yesterday
Basis
Cash price minus the nearby futures price. A negative basis (e.g. −0.18) means the elevator pays 18¢ under futures. Tighter (less negative) basis = better local market.
